Recurring VA Tasks

What a human VA should do — and what AI can take over instead

Context: Ads are currently paused (30–60 days). When they restart, all tasks activate at full volume. This doc separates tasks a human VA must do from tasks AI can handle — so we don't waste VA hours on things that can be automated.
🕐 Time Zone: All scheduling uses Playa del Carmen time (CST / UTC-5). Guru does not observe daylight savings. Mar–Nov: Playa = EST. Nov–Mar: Playa = 1hr behind EST. Always set Google Calendar to CST / UTC-5.

Ad Comment Moderation
Instagram + Facebook · Meta Business Suite · VPN required
Human VA
VA Does Now
  • VPN on first — NordVPN New York Server #12071
  • Go to business.facebook.com → confirm correct page selected
  • Inbox → Instagram comments → Facebook comments — go one by one
  • Reply — genuine comments, human, polite, short
  • Block + Delete — hate, spam, bots, crypto, fake profiles
  • Escalate — anything borderline → send to Ivona + Matt
AI Can Do Instead
  • AI classifies every comment: Reply / Delete / Escalate
  • Auto-deletes spam and bots instantly
  • Drafts replies for genuine comments, VA approves in batch
  • Only truly ambiguous comments reach human review
  • Tools: ManyChat + AI filter, or Meta automation rules
→ VA time cut by ~80% — only reviews escalations
