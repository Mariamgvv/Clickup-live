⚠️ We are not the first "AI sponsor"

Direct AI-sponsor competitors are already live — including one literally named "AI Sobriety Sponsor." The "AI sponsor" idea alone is no longer a unique selling point.

That's not bad news — it means we win on how we deliver it (no app, right in WhatsApp), breadth (every program), and mission (one-for-one), not on being first.

The landscape
Tier 1 · Direct

AI sponsors & recovery chatbots

SoberAI — "AI Sobriety Sponsor"
Multiple AI sponsors with different personalities (compassionate listener, strict motivator, 12-step guide). Our closest mirror.
Loosid — SAM™ AI ("Sobriety & Addiction Mentor")
A "virtual sponsor" with text, voice & video check-ins + craving intervention. Just secured AWS funding; going enterprise (EAPs, treatment centers). Well-resourced.
OpenRecovery — "Kai"
AI assistant that remembers your sponsor / higher power, with SMART Recovery + CBT exercises.
Recomorphosis · Soberfy · Kind Companion
More 24/7 AI recovery coaches, 12-step-rooted.

Where we genuinely stand out
📱 In WhatsApp — no app to download
Every competitor above is a native app (App Store / Play). We live right in the messaging people already use — no download, no new login, no friction.
SoberAI, OpenRecovery, Loosid — all app-gated.
🧭 Every program, not just alcohol
Deep on AA, NA, Al-Anon, OA, GA, DA, UA, ACA, SLAA, BDA — not only alcohol/substance.
Most competitors are alcohol/substance + SMART/CBT only.
🤍 One-for-one give
Subscribe, and someone who can't afford it gets AI Sponsor free.
None of them have this.
✦ Powered by Claude (frontier model)
Conversation quality. Claim carefully — competitors' models are unverified.

Don't claim "cheapest" yet. We have not verified SoberAI / Loosid pricing. Clinical apps (Monument) are clearly pricier — that comparison is safe; the AI apps are not, until confirmed.

Recovery doesn't keep office hours. The hardest moments come late — when meetings are over and you don't want to wake anyone. AI Sponsor is there 24/7, right in your messages. No judgment. Completely anonymous. Free for 30 days.

A 24/7 recovery companion, right in WhatsApp. Built on the 12 steps, always there, completely private. Free for 30 days.

Test first: run T1 (no-app / WhatsApp) against T4 (3am emotion) — that tells us whether differentiation or feeling pulls harder before scaling spend.

⚠️ Meta compliance — read before entering versions

  • Recovery is a Meta "sensitive category."
  • Never write copy that assumes the reader has a condition ("Struggling with addiction?" gets rejected).
  • Describe the help — never label the reader. Every text above invites rather than accuses.
