⚠️ We are not the first "AI sponsor"

Direct AI-sponsor competitors are already live — including one literally named "AI Sobriety Sponsor." The "AI sponsor" idea alone is no longer a unique selling point.

That's not bad news — it means we win on how we deliver it (no app, right in WhatsApp), breadth (every program), and mission (one-for-one), not on being first.

The landscape
Tier 1 · Direct

AI sponsors & recovery chatbots

🌻 Sunflower — AI sponsor "Sam" (biggest threat)
VC-backed: YC + a16z speedrun, raised $4.8M. Grew 200 → 100,000 monthly active users in <6 months. Global & multilingual (EN/ES/JP/KR/PT). Combines an AI sponsor + tracker + CBT; wants to be the "Duolingo of sobriety." Founder ex-Head of Growth at Rupa Health ($5M→$75M).
SoberAI — "AI Sobriety Sponsor"
Multiple AI sponsors with different personalities (compassionate listener, strict motivator, 12-step guide). Closest direct mirror by name.
Loosid — SAM™ AI ("Sobriety & Addiction Mentor")
A "virtual sponsor" with text, voice & video check-ins + craving intervention. Secured AWS funding; going enterprise (EAPs, treatment centers).
OpenRecovery — "Kai"
AI assistant that remembers your sponsor / higher power, with SMART Recovery + CBT exercises. Also runs a financial-assistance fund for those who can't afford it.
Recomorphosis · Soberfy · Kind Companion
More 24/7 AI recovery coaches, 12-step-rooted.

📈 The market is heating up — and funded

Real VC money is flowing into "AI sponsor" products: Sunflower ($4.8M, YC/a16z) and Loosid SAM™ (AWS-funded, going enterprise). That validates the market is real — but it also means we're a bootstrapped product entering a competitive, well-resourced field. Our edge has to be sharp and specific, not "we built an AI sponsor."

⚠️ Meta compliance — read before entering versions

  • Recovery is a Meta "sensitive category."
  • Never write copy that assumes the reader has a condition ("Struggling with addiction?" gets rejected).
  • Describe the help — never label the reader. Every text above invites rather than accuses.
