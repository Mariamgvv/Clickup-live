🎯 Goal
The target you commit to for the month. Set on the 1st Monday. Tip: Customers Goal × Price is a quick starting point.
📊 Forecast
Realistic expectation given current pipeline — usually between Goal and Actual. Lets you spot a miss early.
✅ Actual
What actually landed (reality). Fill at month-end. Variance & Attainment calculate automatically.
Attainment %
Actual ÷ Goal. ≥100 hit · 75–99 close · 50–74 behind · <50 miss.
